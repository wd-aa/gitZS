Huangman

Hero profile:

Huangman, female, Han nationality, born in November 1984, member of the Communist Party of China, university degree, Yulin, Guangxi, is currently the deputy chief of the criminal investigation brigade of the Yuzhou Branch of the Public Security Bureau of Yulin City, Guangxi, and vice chairman(part-time) of the Guangxi Women's Federation. Work as a forensic scientist. In the past 10 years, she has devoted herself to the public security forensic work with solid forensic technology, strong dedication and sense of responsibility. She has spoken for the dead and defended the rights of the living, and defended fairness, justice, and legal dignity. Absolute loyalty cast the soul of the police, always be politically sensible. Huangman firmly and consciously maintains a high degree of consistency in ideological and political actions with the Central Committee of the Party, with Comrade Xi Jinping at the core, and truly ensures that there is a Party, a people, a responsibility and a warning in mind. Write songs of loyalty with fiery youth and magnificent careers. She is not dirty, does not fear odour, and is not afraid of suffering. She has examined more than 600 bodies, helped to crack 498 major and difficult cases, examined more than 4,000 live injuries, and assisted and issued more than 3,200 identification notes. The case uncovered the truth and provided evidence for the prosecution. To add luster to the badge. In the special positions of high strength and high risk, Huangman did not allow her to face the eyebrows and suffered many tests of life and death. She still did not forget to move forward.

Working Attitude

Ai Gang is dedicated to her work and her work. In the face of risks, she has no scruples. Forensic medicine is a high-risk profession. Not only does it have to face the bloody and rancid smell of the scene, but it also deals with bodies with infectious bacteria. It is possible to be infected if there is a slight negligence in the work process..

One day in July 2013, Huangman performed an autopsy on the body of an AIDS infected person. After three or four hours of intense concentration, she felt very tired. As a result, her hand slipped and the suture needle accidentally punctured her finger. After the branch and brigade leaders learned of the situation, she was immediately sent to the Red Cross Hospital in Yulin City to consult on AIDS blocking methods. The doctor told him that he needed to take AIDS blocking drugs for three consecutive months and monitor it for one year before he could confirm whether he was infected with AIDS. After taking the blocking drug, the side effects of the drug made her dizzy and vomiting. In the face of pain and long treatment processes, Huangman actively cooperated with doctors. After repeated examinations, in October 2014, Huangman was excluded from AIDS and returned to her beloved job.

After many years of forensic work, Huangman was deeply aware of the risks and hardships involved in her work, but she said: Ldquo; Forensic medicine is a sacred profession that allows the dead to speak on behalf of the dead. Choose what you love and love what you choose. Since you choose forensic work, you must stick to it.”

Character experience

In the past 8 years, Huangman has performed more than 400 autopsies, more than 2,500 live injuries tests, and issued more than 1,000 identification notes, including 76 on-site investigations and forensic examinations of major and difficult cases, which provided clues for the detection of cases., pointing out the direction of investigation.

On November 25, 2010, the masses reported that: In the ditch of Nanjiang Community in Nanjiang Street, Yuzhou District, Yulin City, a female corpse was found in a weaving bag. When Huangman and his colleagues arrived at the scene, they found that the body's face had been corrupted. After careful examination by Huangman, it was inferred that the deceased was killed and dumped here. The road around the scene was extensive, and no clues were found on the scene. In order to find the first scene as soon as possible, Huangman patiently searched in the wild grass around him and finally found a drop of blood on the grass leaves. She searched carefully for the blood drop, and found an old house a few hundred meters away. There was a pile of burning ashes next to the house.

According to the owner, the house was rented to a man and a woman six months ago two outsiders. The house has been rinsed. The careful Huangman extracted a drop of the deceased's blood from the bottom of an old bicycle shelf, and found a suspect's murder hammer from the bottom of the bedroom bed, providing clues and evidence for locking the suspect..
