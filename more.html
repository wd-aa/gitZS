Outstanding Police Flower Wu Hongtao

In today's era, there are many outstanding women who are making contributions to the society. They have proved by their practical actions that women do not let the elegant demeanour of men.

Wu Hongtao: I would rather wait for the masses than the masses.

Accompanied by loyalty, interacting with dedication and taking pleasure in building a safe side, she has made solid footprints from a household registration police officer to a deputy director, instructor and director of the police station, and then to the deputy director of the public security bureau. She has won four third-class honors. She has been awarded "March 8th Red Flag Bearer" by district, city and provincial governments successively, and has won the honorary titles of "Top Ten Female Heroes" in congtai district, Handan City's "Top Ten People's Police", Handan City's "Top Ten Female Police Officers" and Hebei Province's "Top Ten Female Defenders". Behind these achievements lie many little-known deeds of Wu Hongtao, which is a true portrayal of her loyalty to the Party, the people and the law.

Zhang Min: A Police Flower Blooming in the Front Line of Drug Control

Asphyxiating squats, scary tracking, close cosmetic investigations, and dangerous arrests ... Over the past ten years, she has demonstrated the elegant demeanour of women in anti-drug investigation, anti-drug publicity, preventive education, and management of precursor chemicals with practical actions.
