Wang Congying's "Courageous and Brave Arrest of Fugitive Offenders"

Wang Congying, female, took part in the work in 1987 and is now a long-distance police patrol officer of Songjiazhuang Station Police Station of Beijing Public Security Bureau. Zhao Gongkou Long-distance Passenger Station is one of the police stations under the jurisdiction of Songjiazhuang Station of the Public Transport Corps. It is home to 430 buses from more than 120 regions in 13 provinces, including Henan, Hebei, Shanxi, Shandong, Inner Mongolia, Tianjin, Shanghai, Hubei, Anhui, Zhejiang, Jiangsu, Liaoning and Heilongjiang, with 7,000 to 8,000 passengers per day. Wang Congying works here every day to ensure the safety of the masses.

Wang Congying

Character deeds:

In April 2008, Wang Congying was transferred to Songjiazhuang Station police station to do patrol work. During the patrol, she will bring some small things such as carsickness medicine, candy, sewing kit and so on, in addition to the big police things she must bring with her, such as radio station, baton, handcuffs and so on. In case of passenger motion sickness, sudden hypoglycemia, button drop, etc., it can be used. This careful female police officer has not only won the praise and appreciation of passengers, but also the respect and support of station staff. Once, when interrogating a 1.8-meter-tall man, the man immediately ran as soon as he saw the police. Wang Congying shouted from the station hall to the outside of the station and chased after him. The two security guards on guard at the gate struggled to catch up with the situation and helped Wang Congying to catch the man after running several hundred meters. Through examination, we learned that he was suspected of gathering people to fight and fleeing for 15 years. Distinguish the accent, find out the escaped prisoner ….

Comrade Wang Congying, the honorary editor of the individual, has successively won the first, second and third honors of the individual. He has won such honorary titles as outstanding party members and policemen in the national political and legal system, political and legal policemen satisfying the people of Beijing, the "March 8th" Red Flag Medal, the "good party member in the eyes of the masses" in the political and legal system, and the first "my favorite capital people's police". In 2012, she has been shortlisted for the first batch of official candidates in the "model of loving the people around me" and "public security hero in my heart" selection campaign being carried out by the Ministry of public security. In April 2015, Wang Congying won the honor of "the most beautiful grass-roots public security police" jointly issued by the Central Propaganda Department and the Ministry of Public Security.

Zhao Xiao's "Women Don't Allow Men to Enforce the Law impartially

Zhao Xiao is currently a senior staff member in the Accident Division of Huangdao Brigade, Traffic Police Detachment of Qingdao Public Security Bureau. As a dual-police family, it is conceivable that she faces pressure from work and life. However, she has always been able to find the best balance between work and family, never affecting her work because of family or personal circumstances. Since taking part in the public security work, she has earnestly practiced the purpose of serving the people wholeheartedly, based on her post, worked hard, worked hard, and made silent contributions. She has received unanimous praise and recognition from leaders, comrades and the masses. Hard work, courage to innovate, courage to open up, in ordinary positions made extraordinary achievements.

In the past year, she has participated in more than 20 missions, made more than 80 rapid police calls, handled more than 60 summary procedure accidents, found 2 drunk driving cases, participated in more than 30 interrogations, remand and arraignment of suspects, and worked overtime for more than 160 hours. In addition to the busy work, I have to take care of four elderly people, carefully educate children, and enthusiastically participate in the work of the daughter's school's home committee, so that I can work conscientiously, have a warm and beautiful family and live in perfect order.

She is a model subordinate to leadership, a good partner and elder sister to colleagues, a good "police flower" who is gentle and warm in front of the masses and enforce the law impartially, a good daughter who is considerate and filial in front of her parents, a good mother who is kind and amiable in her daughter's heart, and a good wife who is gentle and virtuous in her husband's heart.
